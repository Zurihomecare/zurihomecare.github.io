What we offer

Clinical care,
at your doorstep

Every service is delivered by a licensed registered nurse, personalised to your specific health needs and care goals.

Post-Surgical Nursing
Expert recovery care after surgery — wound management, IV therapy, pain assessment, and complication monitoring from a qualified OR-trained nurse.
Paediatric Home Care
Specialist nursing for infants, children, and teenagers — medication, growth monitoring, immunisation support, and parental guidance.
Chronic Disease Management
Ongoing monitoring for diabetes, hypertension, cancer, stroke, dementia, HIV/AIDS, Parkinson's, and other long-term conditions.
Post-Natal Care
Compassionate care for new mothers and newborns — lactation support, newborn assessment, maternal recovery, and family education.
Telehealth Consultations
Remote triage, virtual care coordination, and digital monitoring — ideal for diaspora families managing care of loved ones in Kenya.
Wound Care & IV Therapy
Professional wound dressing, IV cannulation, infusion therapy, and blood product administration — clinical precision at home.
Elderly Home Care
Dignified, patient-centred care for the elderly — personal care assistance, mobility support, medication management, and companionship.
Caregiver Training
Professional training for family members and hired caregivers — equipping them with skills to support patients safely at home.
